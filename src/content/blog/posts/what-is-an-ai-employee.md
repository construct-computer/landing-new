Most "AI assistants" live in a chat window. You ask a question, get an answer, and copy the result somewhere else. An **AI employee** is different: it has its own cloud computer, remembers context across sessions, and finishes multi-step work while you are away.

## Chat assistant vs AI employee

| | Chat assistant | AI employee (Construct) |
|---|---|---|
| **Interface** | Thread of messages | Virtual desktop you can watch |
| **Work style** | Suggests what to do | Executes browser, terminal, and apps |
| **Memory** | Session-bound | Persistent sandbox, files, and memory |
| **Output** | Text in chat | Sent email, updated tickets, saved reports |

## What Construct does day to day

Construct logs into a full virtual desktop with a browser, terminal, calendar, task tracker, and audit log. You assign outcomes:

- Research a market and email a PDF report
- Triage inbox and draft replies for approval
- Update Linear issues from Slack messages
- Run a recurring Monday morning competitor scan

The agent plans the steps, uses connected apps (Gmail, Notion, GitHub, and 1,000+ more), and leaves proof in your workspace and audit log.

## When an AI employee makes sense

Choose an AI employee when you need **recurring autonomous work**, **cross-app workflows**, or **proof of execution**. Stick with chat assistants for quick drafting and brainstorming.
