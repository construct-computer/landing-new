Chat assistants changed how we draft emails and brainstorm ideas. They are less useful when the job is **do the thing** — across multiple apps, on a schedule, with an audit trail.

## The execution gap

A chat assistant can tell you how to update a spreadsheet, export a PDF, and post to Slack. Construct opens the browser, runs the export, saves the file to your workspace, and posts the summary — then logs every step.

That gap shows up in three places:

1. **Persistence** — Construct keeps sandbox files and memory across days, not just the current thread.
2. **Integrations** — 1,000+ connected apps the agent acts on directly, not via copy-paste.
3. **Proof** — audit log, sent email, and workspace artifacts instead of chat text alone.
